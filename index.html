FEATURED EXHIBITS

All in This Together

In a few short months, a new disease turned our entire world upside down. We’re still confronting the uncertainty of the COVID-19 pandemic and its disastrous effects. How did we get to this point, and where do we go from here?

Space Endeavour

While we are building Endeavour’s permanent home, a new addition to the Science Center called the Samuel Oschin Air and Space Center, Endeavour is on display in the Samuel Oschin Space Shuttle Endeavour Pavilion.

LEARN MORE

Mission 26: The Big Endeavour

Moving space shuttle Endeavour across the United States was a massive undertaking. Endeavour first flew on the back of a Boeing 747 from Cape Canaveral, Florida to Edwards Air Force Base in California, while making several stops along the way.
